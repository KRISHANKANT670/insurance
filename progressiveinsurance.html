Progressive Insurance: A Comprehensive Overview

Progressive Insurance stands as a prominent entity in the insurance domain, providing an extensive suite of products to cater to the multifaceted needs of its clientele. This encompasses auto, home, renters, commercial auto, small business, motorcycle, and boat policies. Notably, Progressive has emerged as the third largest auto insurer in the United States and is acclaimed as the top carrier in motor insurance as of late 2022.

Core Values

Central to Progressive's ethos are its core values, which champion the significance of:

  • Respecting differences
  • Acting with kindness and caring
  • Treating others as they wish to be treated

These guiding principles, reflective of the "Golden Rule," are instrumental in shaping the company's culture, ensuring an environment that is inclusive, compassionate, and respectful.

Our Goal

Progressive's primary goal is to offer affordable auto insurance with exceptional coverage and outstanding customer service. We leverage innovative technology to enhance our services and meet the evolving needs of our customers.

History and Leadership

Founded in 1937 by Jack Green and Joseph M. Lewis, Progressive has evolved from its modest inception into a leading force within the insurance industry, headquartered in Mayfield Village, Ohio. Today, Progressive is renowned not only for its broad range of products but also for its pioneering efforts in customer-centric solutions and digital innovation.
